Our Story

In 2020, 24.91 million people worldwide suffered from essential tremors, which made performing daily tasks difficult. Hand tremors can be caused by number of conditions including Parkinson’s disease, multiple sclerosis, stroke, and traumatic brain injuries. Because hand tremors can have a negative impact on a person’s life in variety of ways, our team created an intelligent Active Stabilization Mug to meet the drinking needs of these individuals.

What is an Active Stabilization Mug?

The Active Stabilization Mug (ASM) is a product that uses novel motion detection algorithms which are capable of distinguishing between tremors and intentional drinking motions, to only correct for tremors. These corrections are performed near instantaneously through motors embedded in the handle of the mug. This adds a new layer of usability compared to other products available on the market which rely only on passive ergonomic designs and mechanical designs. Existing products on the market fail to provide the “normal” drinking experience that Parkinson’s individuals are seeking, since they correct for all motions including intentional drinking motions. The ASM allows people with Parkinson's disease to easily drink from the mug in the traditional way without having to worry about spilling their beverage. This product will allow those individuals to overcome their limitations and restore a sense of normalcy in their lives and within social situations.
